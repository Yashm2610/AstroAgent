# The Twelve Houses of Astrology

The houses of a birth chart partition the sky into 12 distinct segments, each representing a specific domain of human experience and life area. While signs show *how* planets express themselves, houses show *where* in life these energies manifest.

---

## 1. First House (House of Self / Ascendant)
- **Primary Domain**: Personality, appearance, first impressions, physical body, self-identity, and new beginnings.
- **Key Question**: "Who am I to the world?"
- **Traditional Association**: Aries / Mars.

## 2. Second House (House of Value)
- **Primary Domain**: Personal finances, material possessions, income, values, self-worth, and security.
- **Key Question**: "What do I own and value?"
- **Traditional Association**: Taurus / Venus.

## 3. Third House (House of Communication)
- **Primary Domain**: Intellect, communication, local travel, short journeys, siblings, writing, neighbors, and early education.
- **Key Question**: "How do I think and communicate?"
- **Traditional Association**: Gemini / Mercury.

## 4. Fourth House (House of Home / Imum Coeli)
- **Primary Domain**: Home, family, ancestry, roots, early foundations, maternal figure, and emotional security.
- **Key Question**: "Where do I feel secure and rooted?"
- **Traditional Association**: Cancer / Moon.

## 5. Fifth House (House of Pleasure)
- **Primary Domain**: Creativity, romance, children, self-expression, speculation, hobbies, play, and joy.
- **Key Question**: "How do I play and create?"
- **Traditional Association**: Leo / Sun.

## 6. Sixth House (House of Health and Service)
- **Primary Domain**: Daily work, routines, physical health, self-care, service to others, pets, and practical duties.
- **Key Question**: "How do I serve, work, and maintain my body?"
- **Traditional Association**: Virgo / Mercury.

## 7. Seventh House (House of Partnerships / Descendant)
- **Primary Domain**: Marriage, committed partnerships, business associates, open enemies, contracts, and interpersonal dynamics.
- **Key Question**: "How do I relate to others one-on-one?"
- **Traditional Association**: Libra / Venus.

## 8. Eighth House (House of Transformation)
- **Primary Domain**: Shared resources, joint finances, inheritance, intimacy, psychology, secrets, death, rebirth, and occult studies.
- **Key Question**: "How do I transform and share intimacy?"
- **Traditional Association**: Scorpio / Mars & Pluto.

## 9. Ninth House (House of Philosophy)
- **Primary Domain**: Higher education, philosophy, foreign travel, long journeys, religion, law, ethics, and belief systems.
- **Key Question**: "How do I search for truth and expand my horizon?"
- **Traditional Association**: Sagittarius / Jupiter.

## 10. Tenth House (House of Career / Midheaven)
- **Primary Domain**: Career, public status, reputation, professional ambitions, authority figures, and life calling.
- **Key Question**: "What is my legacy and public standing?"
- **Traditional Association**: Capricorn / Saturn.

## 11. Eleventh House (House of Friendships and Hopes)
- **Primary Domain**: Friendships, networks, groups, associations, humanitarian ideals, hopes, wishes, and future dreams.
- **Key Question**: "What are my dreams and who is my community?"
- **Traditional Association**: Aquarius / Saturn & Uranus.

## 12. Twelfth House (House of the Subconscious)
- **Primary Domain**: Subconscious mind, secrets, dreams, karma, spiritual liberation, isolation, hidden enemies, hospitals, and self-undoing.
- **Key Question**: "How do I connect to the spiritual source and let go?"
- **Traditional Association**: Pisces / Jupiter & Neptune.
